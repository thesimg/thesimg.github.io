Lux

A procedurally generated platformer
Should any loading problems arise, refresh the page.

Use arrow keys or WASD to move.

Use shift to bomb and kill enemies using the bombs.

Click or press ENTER to place scaffolding and DOWN or S to go through it.

Find the portal using the crosshairs around the screen.

Blue: Portal
Green, Orange, and Red: Enemies
White: Crystal

The green enemies take 3 hits to kill, the orange ones 2 hits, and the red ones take one hit.

Collect crystals to earn points

You can take fall damage if you fall to far, so be careful.

You lose health when making scaffolding, dropping bombs, and falling.

Enjoy!
